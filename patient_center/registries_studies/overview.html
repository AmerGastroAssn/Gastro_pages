It can take more than 10 years for a new device to reach the patient’s bedside. That’s too long for patients to wait for innovations that could change lives. That’s why AGA began working as a neutral, objective broker to establish clinical research studies. Through this initiative, AGA collects important data to assess the value of new technologies on patient care. Ultimately, the data AGA collects will support the approval, coverage and adoption of new technologies that demonstrate promise and merit.
