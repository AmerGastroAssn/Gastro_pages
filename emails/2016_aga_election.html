As a voting member of the American Gastroenterological Association, you are invited to vote in the 2016 election.

As required by the AGA Bylaws, the membership was solicited to propose themselves or other eligible members for the AGA Nominating Committee to consider. The nominating committee met on Jan. 30, 2016, and considered all eligible nominees. The committee’s selections are presented in the Councillors Slate and the Nominating Committee Slate.

A ballot is linked below, providing all voting members with the opportunity to vote by April 22, 2016, for candidates for at-large councillor, clinical research councillor, education and training councillor, and for four of the eight candidates for the 2016-2017 AGA Nominating Committee. Election results will be announced at the AGA Annual Business Meeting during DDW® in May.

We are also pleased to announce the 2016-2017 officers. This is provided for your information only, not for voting.
