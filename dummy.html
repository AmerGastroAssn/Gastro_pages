Principles of GI for the NP and PA

The A-Team: Delivering High-Value GI Care
Aug. 19–21, 2016 • Chicago, IL

Don’t miss this opportunity. AGA's highly popular course Principles of GI for the Nurse Practitioner (NP) and Physician Assistant (PA) provides in-depth programming on best practices and evidence-based medicine that will strengthen your diagnostic and therapeutic skill sets.

Expert Instruction
Our expert faculty will provide you with practical instruction on effectively treating multiple GI and hepatic disorders — from IBD to hepatitis C and chronic pancreatitis. Other topics covered include:

  • How NPs and PAs fit into practice paradigms in an era of
    value-based care.
  • Eosinophilic esophagitis — dilate vs. treat?
  • New therapies for Clostridium difficile.

Network and Share 
The free lunch session on day one (Aug. 19) will allow NPs and PAs, new to GI, an opportunity to engage with more experienced course attendees. Participants will be encouraged to freely discuss successes and challenges while working in GI for a shared learning experience.

Visit the course Web page to register and view the program in its entirety.
