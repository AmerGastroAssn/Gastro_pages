Fee Information
There is no fee for this educational activity.

Target Audience
This education program is designed for gastroenterologists and gastroenterology clinicians who manage patients with IBD.

Educational Objectives
After engaging in this educational activity, learners will be to:

  • Compare and contrast the clinical effects of IBD therapies.
  • Select individualized treatments for patients with IBD that maximize the opportunity to achieve remission and avoid relapse while minimizing toxicity.
  • Utilize available disease progression and drug monitoring strategies for patients with IBD.
  • Implement AGA practice measures in managing patients with IBD.
