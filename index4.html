2017 AGA Tech Summit 
April 12–14, 2017 • Boston, MA
 Brought to you in conjunction with SAGES

Connect and engage with physician, payor and medtech leaders at the 2017 Tech Summit, where you will explore the factors and trends that have helped the GI marketplace evolve into an attractive option for investment. You'll learn about opportunities in personalized medicine, evaluating patient risk and improving outcomes. 

This is also a unique opportunity to: 

  • Get an insider's perspective on how GI technology evolves from concept to reality, including what's needed to obtain adoption, coverage and reimbursement. 
  • Learn about initiatives to support innovation in the GI space through the  AGA Center for GI Innovation and Technology.

Sessions will cover the top medtech successes from 2016 and how health-care reform/MACRA will impact the GI environment.

For more information on the program and to register, visit the Tech Summit website.

Don't Miss the 'Shark Tank'
Representatives from startups and large companies, with a focus in GI, will present their novel idea/innovation to an expert panel of industry leaders for feedback.
