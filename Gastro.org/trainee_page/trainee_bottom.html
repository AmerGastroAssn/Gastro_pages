Your Benefits as an AGA Trainee Member

Improve Your Skills

AGA members have exclusive access to free GI SAMs, Post-GTE resources and discounts on electronic and in-person learning opportunities year round.

Stay Up to Date

Membership includes free access to AGA's journals, monthly newspaper, bi-monthly magazine weekly e-newsletter and daily email newsfeed.

 

Connect with Colleagues

Search AGA's member directory, find a mentor through our Mentor and Advisor Program and interact with peers through AGA Community and social media networks.
 

 

Get Published

Opportunities to get published in AGA's journals: Gastroenterology , Clinical Gastroenterology and Hepatology and The New Gastroenterologist .

Advance Your Career

Membership allows access to nearly $1.2 million in research awards and savings on publication fees in CMGH .

 

Prepare for the Future

AGA provides resources to help you navigate your GI training and beyond, including an interactive guide to choosing a clinical practice setting, a guide for going into practice and a compilation of relevant articles.

 

Gain a Voice on Capitol Hill

Participate in AGA advocacy activities and utilize AGA's online advocacy system to make sure legislators and regulatory agencies are meeting the needs of GIs.

Save Money

Take advantage of special promotions through AGA's affinity partners such as UpToDate®, Epocrates® and CommonBond.

Gain Visibility

Join the AGA Young Delegates and represent AGA on a variety of short-term initiatives. Email trainees@gastro.org to learn more.
