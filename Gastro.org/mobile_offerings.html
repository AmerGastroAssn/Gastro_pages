AGA App Central

App Central is your access point to all of AGA’s apps and the exclusive home of DDSEP® 8 and the Clinical Image Challenge. Free to download, App Central also includes the latest news from AGA and a list of upcoming meetings and conferences.

DDSEP® 8

Assess and enhance your GI medical knowledge with this comprehensive collection of case-based questions. The app includes a total of 850 questions covering all major topics in the field of digestive diseases. Whether you’re preparing for the GI board exam, looking to assess your knowledge or keeping current on advances in the field, you can depend on DDSEP® 8 to meet your educational needs.

Image Challenge

Test and hone your diagnostic skills with this extensive collection of images and case studies from the Clinical Challenges and Images in GI section of Gastroenterology. Each challenge includes high-definition images, a detailed case scenario and the correct diagnosis with in-depth rationale and references. Sort and filter by organ, most popular or favorites.

The New Gastroenterologist

Insights for Fellows & Young GIs is the AGA’s newest publication dedicated specifically to the needs of fellows and young career gastroenterologists. This quarterly publication will serve as a resource for not just young GIs, but also for the entire gastroenterology community. The New Gastroenterologist is the first publication of its kind within the field of gastroenterology and presents an exciting opportunity to concentrate content into a single place that specifically addresses the topics and issues that are common to all young career gastroenterologists. The New Gastroenterologist provides high-yield content that can be directly applied to career goals and delivers content in a concise and easily readable format through a variety of features in each issue.
