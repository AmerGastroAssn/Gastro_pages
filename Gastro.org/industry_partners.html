AGA has long been recognized as the leading medical society for gastroenterology. We welcome engagement with corporate partners in emerging areas of science, drug and device development, disease management and current hot topics in GI and thank our current industry supporters.

 

Corporate Roundtable

The AGA welcomes our corporate partners to be members of our corporate roundtable. The roundtable engages corporate representatives and AGA leadership in discussions regarding scientific advancements, policy, legislation and other issues and opportunities that impact the science and practice of gastroenterology and hepatology.
