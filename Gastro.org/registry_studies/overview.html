How to Get Invloved

 

Gastroenterologists

There are several ways you can get involved with AGA's registry initiative:
  • Participate as a research study site.
  • Serve as a national primary investigator or sub-investigator.
  • Serve on a data safety monitoring board.
  • Serve on a registry publications committee.
  • Serve on the AGA Registry Oversight Subcommittee.
  • Suggest registry topics to be explored.

Contact: Sonya Serra, senior director of registry development and integrity
