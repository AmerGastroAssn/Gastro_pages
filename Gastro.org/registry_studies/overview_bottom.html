registry oversight subcommittee

The Registry Oversight Subcommittee maintains direct oversight over all the Center for GI Innovation and Technology observational device registries and associated activities to govern registry creation, management, and closure. This group’s mission is to ensure that the Center’s device registries apply scientific rigor and good clinical practices through its lifespan.
