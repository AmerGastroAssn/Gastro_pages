Discover the many benefits of your first year AGA membership through tips delivered to you monthly. Below is the tip for June 2015. 
As a first-year AGA member, you now have special access to a community of GI professionals who share your interests and understand your needs. You also have access to tools and resources that can help you improve quality, maximize revenue and provide the best care for your patients.

With AGA you can:

  • Thrive in the new world of accountable care. Access practical, easy-to-use information through the AGA Roadmap to the Future of GI and the accompanying Roadmap to the Future of GI Toolbox.
  • Help your practice adapt to an ever changing health-care environment. Learn proven management strategies and best practices at the GI Outlook 2015 (GO 2015). Register by July 9 and save $100.
  • Stay up to date on the latest clinical guidelines. Easily access guidelines for more than a dozen GI-related topics and conditions on any Internet-connected device.
  • Demonstrate excellence to patients and payors. Use the AGA Digestive Health Recognition Program™, and IBD, HCV and the new colorectal cancer screening and surveillance modules, to qualify for the CMS Physician Quality Reporting System, complete MOC requirements and gain recognition for superior quality.
